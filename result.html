SUS Score Overview

📊 System Usability Scale (SUS) — Summary

The SUS is a 10-item questionnaire that produces a score from 0–100. Scores above 68 are considered above average, while scores at or above 80.3 fall in the "Excellent" range. Below are the aggregated results from our respondents.

📋 Individual SUS Scores per Respondent

* SUS Score = Raw Score × 2.5  |  Excellent: ≥80.3  |  Good: 68–80.2  |  Acceptable: 51–67.9

Per-Item Results

📋 SUS Question Scores (Mean Rating)

* For positive items, contribution = mean − 1. For negative items, contribution = 5 − mean. Total SUS = sum of contributions × 2.5.

📊 Mean Ratings per SUS Item (Visual)

Bars represent the mean score (1–5 scale) per question. Shaded bars are negative items where a lower score indicates better usability.
